Chester Charles Bennington

One of the greatest Singer, Songwriter & Actor

Here's a time line of Chester's life:

  • 1976 - Born in Phoenix, Arizona
  • 1987 - Divorce of his parents. His father took custody of him, but barely cared for his son; he also felt abandoned by his mother. The dysfunctional relationship Bennington thus developed with his family culminated in thoughts of killing his family members and running away. He was also subjected to sexual abuse during childhood and adolescence, which he claimed began at the age of seven or eight and did not end until he turned 13. Out of shame and fear of being ridiculed as homosexual, Bennington had confided in someone late in life.
  • 1993 - Formed the Band Sean Dowdell and his Friends (Grey Daze) with his youthfriend Sean Dowdell.
  • 1994 - Chester Bennington graduated.
  • 1995 - Together with Sean Dowdell, he founded a tattoo studio in Phoenix in 1995 and built up a tattoo studio chain in the following years
  • 1996 - Chester has his first son Jamie with his Ex-Girlfriend Elka Brand.
  • 1998 - The band Grey Daze disbanded
  • 1999 - Recieved a call from the former Zomba Music manager Jeff Blue. At that time, Blue was in charge of the band Xero, which was looking for a new singer. Blue asked Bennington to record his vocals over Xero demos. Within three days, Bennington completed all the demos. He then played the demos to Blue via telephone and was subsequently invited to Augora Hills to join Xero. After an application music session, Xero found a new singer in Bennington. The band then named itself Hybrid Theory.
  • 2000 - Eventually, with the help of Jeff Blue, who was by then vice president there, the band found a publisher in Warner Music Group and released their first album under the name Linkin Park in October 2000, which sold over 24 million copies
  • 2005 - Married his wife Talinda Bentley. In order not to further burden the band Linkin Park with his personal problems, Bennington, together with members of Julien-K, formed a solo project in 2005, which went under the name Snow White Tan. This solo project gave birth to the band Dead by Sunrise, which took longer than expected to release its debut album, so it was not released until the fall of 2009. Meanwhile, Bennington had stopped processing experiences from his life in songs by the band Linkin Park, which contributed to a change in the style of their music that was controversial among fans.
  • 2017 - On July 20, 2017, Cornell's birthday, Bennington was found dead at his Palos Verdes Estates mansion, also by suicide, at the age of 41. He was buried near Los Angeles.
